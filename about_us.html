Our Mission

Our goal is to simplify online education by integrating live sessions, student tracking, and secure access through personalized dashboards for students and teachers.

Project Overview

This system uses Jitsi Meet for online classes, Google Forms & Sheets for session tracking, and a dynamic dashboard for each user. It ensures that learning and attendance tracking are both seamless and efficient.
